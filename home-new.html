FAQ
1
Where do I buy tickets?
Actually, you don’t. You can attend DeSci.Berlin free of charge, however there are limited tickets, and you should still need to register and reserve your place. Link to the registration form coming soon!
2
CAN I sponsor DeSci.Berlin?
We're not looking for financial sponsorship for this edition of DeSci.Berlin, but if you or your organisation can contribute value to the event, please get in touch with us using the speaker and partner registration form and indicate your area of expertise.
3
How do i apply to speak at this event?
Here’s a form for speakers to apply. As our event is focused on the educational aspect, we would like to have more workshops, than talks. The form is for applying to both: hosting a workshop and giving a talk.
4
What is the COVID protocol for the event?
Since March 8th 2022, Berlin has lifted many of the restrictions that have been in place through the pandemic. So that means our gathering will proceed "as normal", without social distancing, masks, tests etc. Please do take care when you're in public transportation: wear a mask (mandatory) and keep your hands disinfected to reduce the chance picking up the virus while you're traveling.
